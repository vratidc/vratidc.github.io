## The Project
"Fragments of a Home" is an immersive VR narrative set in a submerged future Earth, where personal memory and planetary collapse intersect.

Blending environmental storytelling with interactive memory fragments, the experience places the viewer inside the perspective of a lone researcher navigating her memories. Through spatial exploration and tactile interaction, it invites us not just to witness the story, but to actively uncover it, and gradually piece it together.

<br>

## Synopsis
> _After surviving a crash, a lone researcher, Siya, awakens inside a damaged pod with fractured memories. Following a clue pointing to her long-lost childhood home, she embarks on a journey with her loyal bot across a drowned future Earth. As past and present begin to overlap, her personal search becomes entangled with the fate of the planet itself, resurfacing memories of her mother and a long-deferred confrontation with grief, legacy and letting go._

<br>

## The Experience
- Format: 6DoF Virtual Reality
- Duration: ~25 mins
- Mode: Single-user immersive experience
- Interaction: Environmental exploration + object-based triggers
